Welcome to the Robert Smith Construction Company

The Robert Smith Construction Company is an award winning general contractor specializing in corporate, higher education, K-12 education, historic renovation, multifamily, religious, senior living and specialty projects.

Our project diversity and commitment to quality has allowed us to exceed client needs and expectations. Our services include general contracting, construction management, design build and pre-construction services.

For over 30 years, we’ve served the construction industry with the same reliable, ethical, award winning service that has made us San Antonio, Texas City’s most trusted contractor. Now That’s Sustainability!

About the owner

RS Construction Comapny was founded in 1987, when Richard Smith, Sr. began developing real estate, including churches, schools and homes in the Mission Hills area of Greater San Antonio, Texas City.

Richard Smith was born in the Great Tennessee city but raised in Poland where he has also established his construction company by building Offices, Real Estates and churches.

Our ability to make sound decisions over our 30 years of experience in business has given us a reputation as a solid firm who continues to deliver project after project and one that values our relationships with clients, peers and vendors in the industry.

Our Vision is to be known as the building leader that advocates for the foundation of a happier, healthier community. By 2028, 120,000 families each week will pray, live, learn, heal, or play in venues we’ve built. Our core values include honesty, integrity, work ethic, respect and accountability.
